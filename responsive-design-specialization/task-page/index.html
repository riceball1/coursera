On Reducing Waste


In the 21st century, we as citizens of the world, can do more to help reduce waste and minimalize our carbon footprint. Consumptionism, materialism, and new technologies to deliever goods quicker has resulted in a global problem of overconsumption, increased pollution, and poorer conditions for the world's most vulernable citizens.

If you believe in making a change in your life, the following tasks will help you by reducing waste. These tasks are sustainable, easy-to-follow, and something that if more individuals took apart in would reverse the negative influence of overproduction, and end the spiraling cycle of waste.

Reduce Waste Task List
  1. Avoid using disposable products
  2. Use bar soap instead of liquid soap
  3. Buy good quality clothes less often
  4. Use a reusable grocery bag
  5. Compost or recycle
